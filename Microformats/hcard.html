Using hCards to Provide vCard Information

The hCard is based on the vCard format and provides downloadable, machine-readable information in a human readable form.

Here is an example Contact me <div>

Contact Me

You can contact me via email to mark.kenyon@irisdesignsolutions.co.uk, or send stuff to me at the following address:

Fieldside,
Lyeway Lane,
Ropley

Here is the code:


<div id="contact">
  <h2>Contact Me</h2>
  <p>You can contact me via email to 
   <a href="mailto:jane@example.com">jane@example.com</a>, 
   or send stuff to me at the following address:</p>
  <p>255 Some Street,<br />
     Some Place,<br />
     Some Town</p>
</div>

The steps needed to provide a hCard are

  1. Add a class ,vcard to the holding <div>
  2. A name is required for the vcard. We use the fn(full name)class to mark it up.
  3. We mark up the email with the email class.
  4. We mark up the whole address within a <div> with the adr class. We then mark up the subsections of the address with the appropriate classes. each within it's own <div>.
    • street-address (the street address).
    • locality (the town).
    • region (the county).

Contact Me

Mark Kenyon

You can contact me via email to mark.kenyon@irisdesignsolutions.co.uk, or send stuff to me at the following address:

Fieldside, Lyeway Lane
Ropley
Hampshire

That is it! Here is the finished code:


  <div id="contact" class="vcard">
    <h2>Contact Me</h2>
    <h3 class="fn">Mark Kenyon</h3>
    <p>You can contact me via email to 
     <a class="email" href="mailto:mark.kenyon@irisdesignsolutions.co.uk">mark.kenyon@irisdesignsolutions.co.uk</a>, 
     or send stuff to me at the following address:</p>
     <div class="adr">
  		<div class="street-address">Fieldside, Lyeway Lane</div>
     	<div class="locality">Ropley</div>
     	<div class="region">Hampshire</div>
     </div>
  </div>

Adding a Download link

You can add a link to allow users to download your contact information e.g.

	<a href="http://h2vx.com/vcf/pageWithhCard.com">Download vCard</a>

This uses the h2vx service to convert your hCard to a vCard for download. An example can be found here:

Download vCard

If the target page has more than one hCard then you can add an id attribute to each and add %23hCardID to the end of the href e.g.

	<a href="http://h2vx.com/vcf/pageWithhCard.com%23hCardID">Download vCard</a>
